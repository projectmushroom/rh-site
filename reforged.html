REFORGED

Reforged Programme: A Journey to Discipline and Purpose

The Reforge Program wasn’t born in a boardroom or a classroom. It began as a response to the hollow leadership advice I saw around me—advice that lacked real-world grit and authenticity.The Reforged Programme took shape during a 10-day silent, plant medicine retreat in Peru, where I stripped away all distractions and focused purely on introspection and nature.

21-Day Rite of Passage for Men and Woman Ready to Rebuild

Reforged
A modern rite of passage for men and women in their 30s and 40s who’ve faced adversity and want to rebuild silently—with discipline, purpose, and code.

THE PROBLEM

Modern adults are drowning in noise, distraction, and unprocessed pain.

  • Burnout. Disconnection. Softness.
  • Therapy is useful—but many men and woman crave action, clarity, and systems.
  • No more fluffy personal development. Men need a reset built on code.

THE SOLUTION

Reforge Programme
A 21-day protocol that rebuilds through rhythm, systems, and alignment.

Not coaching. Not a retreat. Not therapy.
It’s a solo rebuild, backed by community, built for those who walk through fire.

 

 

THE STRUCTURE

3 Phases | 21 Days | One Code

  1. Rhythm (Days 1–7)
    • Reclaim your energy. Install rituals that anchor you.
  2. Leverage (Days 8–14)
    • Burn what doesn’t work. Build systems that scale your peace.
  3. Character (Days 15–21)
    • Align with your internal code. Lock in direction

PHASE OUTCOMES

Rhythm Outcome: 3 grounded rituals that create stillness and focus
Leverage Outcome: A stripped-down weekly system with high ROI
Compass Outcome: Personal Code Blueprint + 3 non-negotiable commitments

PHILOSOPHIES

Built on:

  • The 80/20 Rule (Richard Koch)
  • The 4-Hour Workweek (Tim Ferriss)
  • Ancient fasting + nervous system science
  • Atomic Habits (James Clear)
  • Brotherhood values from La Cosa Nostra: respect, honor, silence
