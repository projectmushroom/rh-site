THE SHIFT 

Fire Built the System. Silence Gave It a Soul.

I spent 10 days in silence at a retreat in the Peruvian Jungle.
No talking. No contact. Just introspection, pain, and truth.

The Reforge Program wasn’t born in a boardroom or a classroom. It began as a response to the hollow leadership advice I saw around me—advice that lacked real-world grit and authenticity, personal brands and leadership keynotes with no soul! 

Leadership and code in my world has always been from a totally different perspective than the "norm" , I have always questioned the status quo and looked at life and leadership in my own way, so many guru's and leadership experts with their own methodologies and coaching systems which mostly is bullshit jargon and stuff they read in a book but never actually practiced and I quickly realized most of these guru's havent lead a team in their lives yet we pay money to watch their carefully crafted keynotes and listen attentively to their super uninsightful insights! This got me thinking that surely after all I have survived all the near death experiences, all the teams I have actually lead to success I want to formulate my own Code my own system of values to follow based on real life experience and real research. After mulling over this in my head and 100 different variations I took a trip to Iquitos Peru to a 10-day silent, plant medicine retreat. This is where tall my thoughts and ideas really took shape and started to make sense, when you have no phone, no talking, very little food and only nature to guide you, where all life's busy bullshit is stripped away and all distractions are gone you have no choice but to focus purely on introspection and nature, back to basics! 

That’s where The REFORGED Programme was solidified!

You don’t build legacy by grinding.
You build it by aligning.
And you don’t lead others until you’ve faced your own shadow.
