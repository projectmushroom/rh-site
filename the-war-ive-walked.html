BUILT IN FIRE

From the Drakensberg to Dubai, from small town to multi-million dollar exits—I’ve lived a life most people wouldn’t survive. I don’t just talk leadership. I’ve bled for it!

 

I grew up in the shadow of the Drakensberg mountains, then moved to "the big city" Johannesburg, Krugerdorp actually! with no formal degrees and no shortcuts. I started my first business at 20 and quickly found myself in the corporate world, leading teams across the globe. My journey hasn’t been easy—I’ve faced addiction, multiple near-death experiences, and the hard lessons of life. But every challenge taught me resilience, humility, and the true meaning of leadership: serving a purpose greater than yourself. Today, I bring all of that experience to the table to help others build, scale, and lead with authenticity and purpose.

Skateboarding was my first mentor. It taught me pain tolerance, creativity, and how to fail ten thousand times with no applause. That mindset followed me into business. I’ve built eight-figure companies from zero. I’ve lost it all and rebuilt with nothing but discipline and an internal code.

This isn’t a personal brand. This is a life lived. And now I use every scar, every lesson, and every ounce of fire I’ve earned to help others scale businesses, lead with courage, and get back up when the world knocks them out.
